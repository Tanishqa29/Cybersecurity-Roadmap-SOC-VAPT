## 🔹 Types of Network

### ▪️ PAN (Personal Area Network)
An ultra-small network used for personal devices within a short range (1–10 meters). It is mainly used for personal data sharing.

**Example:** Bluetooth connection between phone and earbuds  

---

### ▪️ LAN (Local Area Network)
A network that covers a small geographical area like a single room, home, or building. Devices are connected using cables or wireless for short-range communication.

**Example:** Home or office network  

---

### ▪️ WLAN (Wireless LAN)
A type of LAN that uses wireless communication instead of cables. It provides flexibility and mobility to users.

**Example:** WiFi network in a café  

---

### ▪️ CAN (Campus Area Network)
A network formed by connecting multiple LANs within a limited geographical area like a campus, corporate office, or government organization.

**Example:** College campus network or business park network  

---

### ▪️ MAN (Metropolitan Area Network)
A network that covers a city or large urban area. It connects multiple LANs and CANs.

**Example:** Internet services across a city like Mumbai  

---

### ▪️ WAN (Wide Area Network)
A network that covers very large geographical areas like countries or continents. It connects multiple networks together.

**Example:** The Internet
